Baking Tips for Every Baker

Perfect your skills with these helpful baking tips!

1. Basic Baking Techniques

  • Measuring Ingredients: Always use the correct measuring tools for dry and wet ingredients.
  • Mixing Methods: Folding: Gently combine light ingredients (like whipped cream).
  • Sifting Dry Ingredients: Sift flour and other dry ingredients before adding them to your mix.

2. Common Baking Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

  • Overmixing Dough: Overmixing can lead to dense, tough results.
  • Not Using Room Temperature Ingredients: Cold butter or eggs can lead to uneven mixing.
  • Underbaking: Use a toothpick to check if your cakes or muffins are done.

3. Quick Fixes and Time-Saving Tips

  • Batch Baking: Double or triple your favorite dough or batter and freeze for future use.
  • Prep Ahead: Prepare dough or batter in advance, and refrigerate or freeze for quick baking later.
  • Use a Stand Mixer: A stand mixer saves time and gives consistent results, especially for larger batches.

4. Ingredient Substitutions

  • Egg Substitutes: Use applesauce, mashed banana, or flax seeds (1 tbsp flax meal + 3 tbsp water) for vegan or allergy-friendly options.
  • Dairy Alternatives: Replace milk with almond or oat milk, and butter with coconut oil or vegan butter.
  • Flour Alternatives: Use almond flour, coconut flour, or gluten-free blends for gluten-free baking.
  • Sugar Substitutes: Try using honey, maple syrup, or stevia for a healthier option.

5. Burnt Bottoms

Problem:
The bottom of your baked goods is burnt while the top is fine.

Solution:

  • Use a baking sheet with a light-colored surface to prevent excessive heat absorption.
  • Try lowering the oven temperature by 10–20°C and bake for a longer time.
  • Consider using an oven thermometer to ensure the oven's temperature is accurate.
