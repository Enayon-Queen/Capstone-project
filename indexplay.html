FAQ

Q: How many teaspoon of baking powder can I use for 500g of flour?

A: You can typically use 3 teaspoons of baking powder.

Q: What are the dietary recommendations for eggs?

A: One egg per day without increasing the risk of heart disease.

Q: How many minutes does it take to bake a cake?

A: 25 to 45 minutes in a standard oven at 350°F (175°C).
