Welcome to the world of pastries!!!

Pastry refers to a variety of doughs, typically made with flour, fat, and water, and also the baked goods made from them. These baked goods can be sweet or savory and are characterized by their flaky and crumbly texture. Common examples include pies,doughtnut,puff-puff and so on......

INGREDIENT

20 spring roll wrapper,500g minced meat,
1 big cabbage,7 medium size carrots,
2 medium size onions,2 handful green peas,
4 medium size green pepper,salt,vegetable oil,
frying pan,seasoned cube,curry,thyme,water

HOW TO PREPARE

STEP 1:

Wash and slice the carrots,onions,cabbage
and green pepper in a seperate plates and
set all aside.wash also the green peas.

STEP 2:

Boil your minced meat with 1 seasoned cube
curry and thyme.set it aside.

STEP 3:

Boil the cabbage till it is soft.then add the
carrots and green peas,add 1 seasoned cube and
cook until the carrots is soft.Then drain the
water out.

STEP 4:

Heat up some vegetable oil on medium heat.fry
the meat.Add curry,thyme and red dry pepper and
mix properly.Add the cabbage,carrots,green peas
and green pepper.stir fry it and set it aside.

STEP 5:

mix some 3 teaspoons of flour with hot water in a
cup and stir properly with a spoon until it is
dissolve.Then set it aside.

STEP 6:

Spread the spring roll wrapper on a flat surface
and fill it with the filling,fold it together
and seal the edge with the liquid flour as seen
below.

STEP 7:

Do these for the remaining spring roll.

STEP 8:

Fry the spring rolls in a dee[p oil until it is
brown and crusty.

STEP 9:

Your Spring roll is raedy.
